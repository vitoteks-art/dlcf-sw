# 2) User Guide (Logged-In Users)

## 2.1 Login
1. Click Portal in the public navbar.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click Login.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/login-page.png

## 2.2 Dashboard sidebar
When logged in, you see a sidebar with links like:
- Attendance Portal
- GCK Attendance
- STMC
- Zonal Congress
- State Congress
- Retreat
- Profile
- Biodata

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/dashboard-sidebar.png

## 2.3 Biodata form
1. Click Biodata.
2. Fill all fields.
3. Click Submit.

Field meanings:
- Full Name: your correct name.
- Gender: male or female.
- Age: your age.
- Phone: your phone number.
- Email: must match your login email.
- Profile Photo: a photo URL.
- School: your school name.
- Category: student, staff, corper, etc.
- Worker Status: member or worker.
- Membership Status: member, worker, associate coordinator, or guest.
- Work Units: units you serve in (for example, Choir).
- Address: your home address.
- Next of Kin: emergency contact.
- State, Region, Cluster, Fellowship: your location.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/biodata-form.png

## 2.4 State Congress registration
1. Click State Congress.
2. Fill the form.
3. Select the Day (Day 1, Day 2, etc.).
4. Click Submit.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/state-congress-form.png

## 2.5 Zonal Congress registration
1. Click Zonal Congress.
2. Fill the form (include Institution).
3. Select the Day.
4. Click Submit.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/zonal-congress-form.png

## 2.6 Retreat registration
1. Click Retreat.
2. Fill the form.
3. Click Submit.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/retreat-form.png

## 2.7 STMC registration
1. Click STMC.
2. Fill the form (Level, Matric Number, Institution, Gender).
3. Click Submit.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/stmc-form.png

## 2.8 Attendance (Weekly Attendance Portal)
1. Click Attendance Portal.
2. Choose your state, region, and fellowship.
3. Choose the service day.
4. Enter numbers for adults, youths, and children.
5. Click Submit.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/attendance-form.png

## 2.9 GCK Attendance
1. Click GCK Attendance.
2. Choose state, region, fellowship.
3. Add sessions (date and period).
4. Enter attendance.
5. Click Submit.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/gck-form.png

# 3) Admin Guide (Administrators)

## 3.1 Admin login
1. Login with an admin account.
2. Click Admin in the sidebar.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-dashboard.png

## 3.2 Users
Add user:
1. Go to Users tab.
2. Fill name, email, password.
3. Choose role.
4. Assign state, region, fellowship (if needed).
5. Select work units (if needed).
6. Click Create User.

Edit user:
1. Find user in list.
2. Click Edit.
3. Update fields.
4. Click Save.

Delete user:
1. Click Delete.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-users.png

## 3.3 Locations
States:
1. Go to Locations.
2. Add or edit states.

Regions:
1. Pick a state.
2. Add regions.

Fellowships:
1. Pick state and region.
2. Add fellowship centers.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-locations.png

## 3.4 Organization
Institutions:
1. Pick a state.
2. Add institutions for that state.

Work Units:
1. Add units like Choir, Media, Publication.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-organization.png

## 3.5 Roles
1. Go to Roles.
2. Add or edit roles.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-roles.png

## 3.6 State Updates
1. Go to State Updates.
2. Select a state.
3. Fill title and content.
4. Choose status (Draft or Published).
5. Click Save.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-state-posts.png

## 3.7 State Home
1. Go to State Home.
2. Select a state.
3. Update hero, stats, events, gallery, sections.
4. Click Save.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-state-home.png

## 3.8 State Congress settings
1. Go to State Congress.
2. Set Start Date and End Date.
3. Click Save.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-state-congress.png

## 3.9 Zonal Congress settings
1. Go to Zonal Congress.
2. Set Start Date and End Date.
3. Click Save.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-zonal-congress.png

## 3.10 Media (Admin)
1. Go to Media tab.
2. Fill title and type (audio or video).
3. Upload media file or paste URL.
4. Upload thumbnail (optional).
5. Choose scope (Zonal or State).
6. Choose status (Published).
7. Click Add.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-media.png

## 3.11 Publications (Admin)
1. Go to Publications tab.
2. Fill title and type.
3. Upload PDF.
4. Upload cover image.
5. Add rich text content (with images).
6. Choose scope (Zonal or State).
7. Choose status (Published).
8. Click Add.

Screenshot: docs/screenshots/admin-publications.png

# 4) Glossary (Simple Meanings)
- Member: a regular DLCF participant.
- Worker: a serving member.
- Associate Coordinator: assistant leader.
- Guest: a visitor.
- Scope: who should see the item (Zonal or State).
- Published: visible to the public.
- Draft: not visible to the public.

---

# 5) Troubleshooting

## I cannot see my media or publication
Check if status is Published.

## Reports show zero
Check date settings and ensure registrations exist for those dates.

## Login not working
Check email and password. Ask admin to reset.
